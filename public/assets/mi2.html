    Bezel-less Metal Build

    Housed in an elegant metal frame, the Xiaomi L40M7-EAIN HD Smart TV lets you watch content on a full screen, ensuring an enjoyable experience.

    Featuring Dolby Audio that offers 20 W high-fidelity sound, this TV is sure to enhance your aural experience. So, no matter what kind of content you're watching, you'll be immersed and entertained by this TV's high-quality sound.

    FHD Display

    Featuring a FHD display, this TV lets you enjoy vivid visuals and deep colours with a rich colour gamut that displays a myriad of colours.

    With its exclusive picture engine, this TV fine-tunes contrast, colours, and depth with every visual, bringing visuals to life.

    Customised Content

    Android 11

    Integrated with the Android Operating System, this TV lets you select from a plethora of apps, cast your favourite content, and do much more.

    Seamless Connectivity

    Sporting Bluetooth 5.0, dual-band Wi-Fi, and HDMI 2.0, this TV provides a stable connection at all times.

    High Performance

    Thanks to the Quad-core A55 chip, this TV delivers a smooth viewing experience. So, you can enjoy watching your favourite content without any hindrance.
